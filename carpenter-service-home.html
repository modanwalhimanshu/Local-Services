Hiring guide for Carpenter Service

A lot of energy and time is invested in keeping a house together – to constantly treat the minor breakdowns and malfunctions. There might be a squeaky door hinge that needs your attention or the vintage master bed may need some fixing. A window frame might need some redoing or you may want a new custom made a wooden shelf for the room. But in today’s rat race, it becomes a task to get these things done. Taking a day off is impractical while fetching a carpenter on Sundays is itself a task. It’s particularly tough to find carpenters in south Delhi. This is where Vedasolution becomes your best friend. Get your work done at the tap of a button and save yourself from the hassle of searching or bargaining. We are here to provide you with the best carpenter services in Delhi at standardised prices.

How it works?

Open the Vedasolutionwebsite or App to request for the service. You just need to fill in your details like the address where the service in need along with the date and time. Once you are done with this process, the best professional available in your area and your chosen time will be allotted. In a time span of 45 minutes, the expert will be at your doorstep. The pricing is standardised and will be calculated on the work hours according to the rates listed on the App.

What is offered?

Professionals associated with Vedasolution are there to provide you with basic carpentry services. This generally includes door repairs, making of new furniture, construction of door and window frames in new houses, construction of wooden slabs, dismantling or mantling of furniture and so on.

Why Vedasolution?

This is the reason that all our services remain on our customer’s discretion, for example, their preferred time frame and so on. Carpentry services are no exception to his format.
• Verified experts: Professionals on the Vedasolution portal are taken through a number of security checks before they are brought on board. There are a number of parameters on which their expertise is checked and are even trained to provide best services.

What if I need a carpenter for the whole day, how will I be charged in that case?
The customer is free to discuss the scenario with the expert, who will then provide a quotation. We make sure that the price quoted by the professional is reasonable and generally less than the market prices.
Do I also need to specify and explain the work before the carpenter reaches?

Generally, the professional contacts the client before reaching the venue so that he can get his tools akin to the requirements of the work. It is here that you can let him know and discuss about any diversions in the work so that he can prepare himself likewise.

How will I know that I am being charged correctly?

The charges are calculated on a standardised rate card which is available on both the website and the App. This rate card has been generated after a close analysis of the market rates as well as keeping the quality provided in mind.
