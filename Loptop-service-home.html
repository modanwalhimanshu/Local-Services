Laptop Service and Repairs
Hiring guide for Laptop Services

A broken laptop can become a struggle, especially when we’ve started taking our work home, and college projects have become more digital. An Even educational sector, today, functions with research and PDF. Hence, be it a professional personnel or a college goer, everyone needs a laptop. This is the reason that a broken laptop can become a nightmare. If you are facing such a situation and have no time to locate a laptop service centre of a particular brand or visit one, we are here to fix this situation for you. Vedasolution laptop repair services provide you with doorstep pickup and delivery. We provide services for all major brands. Hence, be it Lenovo laptop repair, Dell laptop repair, Sony laptop repair, HP laptop repair or Acer laptop repair, we ace them all.

How it works?

As soon as you open the Vedasolution App or website to search for laptop repair service, a pop-up box will appear before you. The set of question is designed to understand your needs and provide you with a service akin to your requirements. Just answer which part/parts need repairing, your location, preferred time and contact number. A professional will be at your doorsteps to pick up your device at your selected time.

Services offered

• Trackpad: Choose this service if your trackpad/touchpad isn’t responding. The clicks are getting registered where you don’t want them.
• Display: Tick against this service if there is a problem with the display such as a distorted display, unresponsive screen and so on.
• Keyboard: If certain keys on the keyboard aren’t working, select this service.
• Motherboard: Tick against this service if your laptop keeps getting shut down after a couple of minutes or if you are facing any issues related to the motherboard such as virus infliction or increased Windows errors.
• Others: Select this service for any other service apart from the above listed ones.
• Battery: Select this service if the battery isn’t working or if the laptop is getting drained way too fast.
• Hard drive: Avail this service for hardware issues such as blue screens, floating pixels etc.
• Software (OS): If you are dealing with software related issues, this service is your go-to.
• Speaker/Mic: Choose this service to get your speakers or mic repaired.

Why Vedasolution

• Insurance: We take full responsibility for your belongings and, hence, we provide an insurance of Rs. 5,000 against damages.
• Verified experts: Each and every professional on Vedasolution is taken through verification and security check to ensure good quality work.
• Post-paid Service: Pay after your device is delivered at your doorstep and you are fully satisfied with the service.
• Online Payment: The payment will be made online. A link will be sent to you via SMS and/or email which will take you to the online payment portal.

Do you provide any kind of warranty?
Yes! The warranty provided varies from brand to brand and service to service. The replacement parts used are 100% genuine and their warranty may range between 3-6 months.
How much time will it take?

The time span of the laptop repair and service entirely depends upon the kind of service availed. If the repair is a minor one, the laptop will be delivered in a day. However, major repairs might take a day or two.

Is my data safe with you?

Don’t stress yourself out over security reasons as your safety is always on the top of our list. Your data is 100% safe with us.
