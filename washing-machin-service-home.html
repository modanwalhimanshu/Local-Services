Washing machine Service and Repairs
Hiring guide for Washing Machine Repair

Essentials appliances like washing machines enable a smooth functioning of our daily chores, making them faster and convenient. With an appliance like washing machine breaking down, it can bring your daily schedule to a standstill. Finding a reliable professional to fix it is an added headache. With this in mind, Vedasolution came up with the idea of providing service for washing machine repair so that our customers are able to hire a professional anywhere and anytime, that too according to their convenience.

How it works?

Go on to the Vedasolution website or app to search ‘washing machine repair’ in the search tab. A pop-up box will open up before you containing few questions. These questions aim to extract information such as the brand of your washing machine, type of washing machine, your locality and time, the kind of service required and so on. As soon as you finish answering the questions, your request will be uploaded on the portal and a professional will be at your doorstep at your requested time and location.

Services offered

• Installing: Tick against this option if you need a professional to install a new washing machine.
• Uninstalling: Choose this service option if you want help with uninstalling an already installed washing machine.
• Washer spinning abnormally: Select this service option if you feel something is not right with the washer of the machine and needs an inspection from a professional.
• Machine making sound: Choose this service option if your machine is making a rattling sound.
• Other: Select this option for any issue not listed above.

Why Vedasolution

• Verified professional: All the professionals on the UrbanClapVedasolution portal are taken through screening for verification. They are even given extensive training to ensure that only good quality of work reaches our customers.
•Online payment: To any risk of frauds and to get away with the unnecessary hassle, Vedasolution promotes the online mode of payment. After the service, a link would be sent to you via SMS and/or email. You just need to click on the link to make the payment.

How much will I be charged?
The professional will be able to advise the recommended service and final quote after detecting the fault with the appliance. You can choose to go ahead with the service or deny it. If you don’t avail any other service except inspection, you will be entitled to pay an inspection charge of Rs. 299.
What is the quality of the spare parts?

We provide 100% authentic and genuine spare parts which are procured from the company itself. For example, for LG washing machine repair, we would procure the parts from LG itself. However, we even have a 30-day service guarantee in case you face any problem.

I have an old top load whirlpool model, is it still worth repairing?

Regardless of the model, you can find expert professionals for Whirlpool washing machine repair. On close inspection, the professional will be able to recommend the best possible solution.
