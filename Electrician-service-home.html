Hiring guide for Electrician Services

Now decades since the invention of electricity, it is just impossible to even imagine even 10 minutes without it. Over so many years our need for electricity has multiplied and diversified. Any small breakdown or malfunction can affect our everyday schedule, jolting it to a standstill. In today’s time where 24 hours don’t seem enough, it becomes difficult to extract time to get such problems fixed. Even searching for a reliable electrician becomes a task. At the same time, it remains highly unsafe to leave those wiring or faulty switches unattended. If you find yourself in such situation, then Vedasolution is the one stop that provides a solution for all your problems. Vedasolution serves as a platform that connects you to the best professionals in near place according to the time convenient to you.

How it works?

As soon as you open the website or the Vedasolution App, a pop-up window will ask your address and the date and time of service. Filling these details is the only task that you have to do. Once this is done, Vedasolution will take charge and find the best professional available in your area at your preferred time. You will have an expert at your doorstep on your selected time. The pricing is calculated on the basis of a standardised rate card available on both the website and the App.

What is offered?

Professionals at Vedasolution provide you with basic electrical repairs such as fixing the wiring system or fans, switches, fuses, fancy light or meters. Whether it is a small repair or an appliance installation, Vedasolution has you covered. Even shifting to a new space will be stress-free with our expert to take care of all your electrical needs while keeping your safety in mind.

What are the inspection charges?
In case the expert was called and no services were availed, you will be entitled to pay him the visiting charge of Rs. 99.
Is there any other way to calculate the price if I want to hire professional for long durations?

If the professional is needed for longer duration such as for a day, you can talk to him about the same. In this case, he will provide you with a quotation. We make sure that this quotation is less than the market price.

Do you carry out both domestic and industrial work?

Yes! Space is no bar to your electrical needs at Vedasolution as we provide services to all kinds of varied customers.
